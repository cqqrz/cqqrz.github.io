Canon Color imageCLASS MF753Cdw

Unscannable Areas / Scan Margins / Cut-off Edges when Scanning

For scanning QSL cards and other things I need to be able to scan all the way to the edge of the paper. That's one main reason why I stayed away from purchasing a Brother multi function printer as their scanners do have a 3mm margin. Brother acknowledges this on their help site, just search for "unscannable".

The first thing I did when I got the MF753Cdw was installing the latest "Master Setup (Windows)" driver from Canon. Shockingly I noticed that the scanner also crops nearly 3mm on the edges when scanning on the flat bed.

The multi function driver "[Windows 64bit] MF753Cdw/ MF751Cdw MFDrivers (Generic Plus UFR II / Generic FAX / ScanGear)" also crops the scan as seen below.

Luckily my daughter setup the printer on another Windows 10 computer and scanned successfully to the edge. It took me quite a while to determine that it was the Canon driver that are faulty and add this limitation. The below scan is what any normal consumer would expect.

The problem now is that with the Microsoft driver the advanced features are lost.
