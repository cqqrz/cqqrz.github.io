Antenna Rotator

Alliance Tenna Rotor

The various models are hard to identify. Controllers and rotators are not always compatible with each other. This table may help with the identification. More details can be found in the service manual.

Controller Rotor
Model Rotation Speed Actuated By Direction Display Number of Terminals Number of Terminals Weather Shield Color Position indication via Has Break Interchangability
T-10 two RPM (one rpm with RCR Kit A) button bar Voltmeter (visible only during rotation) 4 4 red potentiometer yes T-12 (T-10 rotors manufactured before January 1954 need potentiometer upgrade)
U-83 two RPM (one rpm with RCR Kit B rotation knob dial 4 4 gray switch that counts rotation of motor yes U-98
K-22 one RPM (models before serial number K54001 are two and may be convertet with RC Kit B) button bar none, indicator light illuminates when end of rotation limit is reached 4 4 black limit switch unknown (probably yes) F-4
T-12 one RPM (models before serial number T1122001 are two and may be convertet with RC Kit B) button bar Voltmeter (visible at all times) 4 4 red potentiometer unknown (probably yes) T-10 (T-10 rotors manufactured before January 1954 need potentiometer upgrade)
U-98 one RPM (models before serial number U850501 are two and may be convertet with RC Kit A) rotation knob dial 4 4 gray switch that counts rotation of motor yes U-83
T-20 one RPM button bar Voltmeter 4 4 brown (T-20 stampt on housing) potentiometer yes not compadible with other models
U-100 one RPM rotation knob dial 4 4 blue (U-100 stampt on housing) switch that counts rotation of motor yes not compadible with other models
T-45 one RPM button bar Voltmeter 5 5 (more accurate position reading) yellow (T-45 stampt on housing) potentiometer yes (since it's nearly identical to T-20) not compadible with other models
C-225 unknown (probably one RPM) rotation knob red indicator light that it's moving 5 5 green (C-225 stampt on housing) potentiometer unknown (probably yes) C-225A
C-225A unknown (probably one RPM) rotation knob red indicator light that it's moving 5 5 green (C-225 stampt on housing) potentiometer unknown (probably yes) C-225
